Friendships are a funny thing. In the past.... and I mean wayyy back in the early days of mankind, friends were used as a tool for survival.

Friends were used as allies. At the most basic level, they helped navigate the elements and surroundings, they helped imporve reproductive success and most importantly, helped you live longer!

Nowadays we have a more structured society and new technology to help with the survival part. So if we've got that covered.... what's the point of having friends?

Well let me tell you! Humans are social cretures. Friends provide security and support, a sense of belonging through connection based on similar values and interests. They provide meaningful exchanges both creatively and intelectually. In short: Friends are super important!
